Rooted in Care (Sanicare & Haribon)

Sanicare restores ancestral forests in Tanay, Rizal with Haribon Foundation.

Location: Tanay, Rizal & nearby forest sites
Duration: 2014 – Present
Impact:
  • 1,250 native seedlings planted in 2025
  • 15,000+ seedlings planted since 2014
  • 12 hectares reforested
  • 93% seedling survival rate
  • Support for indigenous communities
Goals:
  • Restore ancestral ecosystems
  • Preserve local culture and forest heritage
  • Plant indigenous species (Narra, Pili, Igan)
  • Strengthen soil health and forest resilience
  • Promote long-term rehabilitation
